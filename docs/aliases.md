# Shell Aliases

Generated by `dfm helpers docs-aliases`. Lists aliases from `config/alias`
(bash and zsh) and `config/fish/alias.fish` (fish). Shared aliases come
first; aliases unique to a shell group follow under their own heading.
For simplicity, fish abbreviations (`abbr`) are included as fish aliases,
since they expand to a whole command. Fish `function` definitions that back
an alias (e.g. `.c`, `cdgr`) are listed via their `--wraps` command or, if
absent, their `--description`. Standalone utilities (e.g. `x ip`,
`x ips`) and macOS-specific commands (e.g. `x macos-show`,
`x macos-flushdns`) live in `local/bin/x-*` instead of an alias, so they
are the same in every shell and are not listed here.
